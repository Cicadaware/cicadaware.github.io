    Technological Philosophy

    Do you ever wonder about the impact of technology on humanity? Technology is at every corner in modern society. This poses some issues, people attempt to seek refuge from the constant barrage of information. However, we must talk about the structure of technology within society even before talking about what emerges from the implementation of technology.

    Granular composition framework: the mind, humanity, society, power, war, technology, the future

    The body: Millions and millions of cells, different kinds of tissue and organss

    The brain: combination of neurons, form different areas, houses different parts of mind

    Society: Connected individuals form unique cliques: family clique, work clique, local area clique, city clique, country clique, continent clique - overlapping cliques - inter-clique conflict

    Humanity: large scale inter-clique conflict = war, cliques yearn for power and control both inter-clique and intra-clique. recursive cliquing.

    War: battle of the cliques, “…is a necessary evil”- whoever coined the term is a moron, an exercise in complacency to evil. how to remedy? find overlapping clique between two cliques in conflict.

    Technology: social media, virtual cliques. very rigid structure of technology pushes towards formation of cliques.

    The future: a cyclical dynamic, aim to form the largest collection, setbacks: collections break, shrink, even eliminated, homogenous cliques = as much similar attributes as possible.
